Inventory Features

Complete warehouse intelligence, built in

⚡ Sub-Second Channel Sync

When a sale is made on any channel, MarketMate updates stock levels across every other channel within seconds. Our event-driven sync engine processes changes as they happen — not in batches — so you never suffer an oversell.

🏭 Multi-Warehouse & 3PL Support

Model your exact fulfilment network: internal warehouses, Amazon FBA, third-party logistics providers, dropship suppliers. Each has its own stock pool, and MarketMate routes orders to the right fulfilment source automatically.

📍 Bin Rack Location Management Q3 2026

Create a detailed bin location hierarchy (zone → aisle → rack → shelf → bin) within each warehouse. Assign products to specific bins, track quantities per location, and generate pick paths that minimise walk time.

📈 Demand Forecasting Q3 2026

MarketMate analyses sales velocity and seasonal patterns per product to predict when stock will run out. Forecasts are updated daily and displayed on the Forecasting Dashboard alongside replenishment recommendations.

🔁 Auto-Reorder & Purchase Orders Q3 2026

Set reorder points and preferred supplier per product. When stock falls below the threshold, MarketMate automatically raises a draft purchase order ready to approve and send. Receive goods against the PO to update stock instantly.

🔢 Stock Counts & Adjustments Q3 2026

Run full or partial stock counts against specific locations or product ranges. MarketMate highlights discrepancies between system and physical stock for investigation before committing the count.

📤 Pick Waves Q3 2026

Group orders into pick waves optimised by zone, carrier collection time, or custom criteria. Assign waves to warehouse staff and track pick progress in real time. Generate wave manifests and pack lists with one click.

🔄 Inter-Warehouse Transfers

Create stock transfer requests between warehouses. Track transfer status from created through in-transit to received. Stock is reserved at the source location until the transfer is confirmed at the destination.

📦 Amazon FBA Inbound

Create and manage FBA inbound shipment plans directly in MarketMate. Generate FNSKU labels, declare shipment contents, and track inbound shipment status — without leaving the platform.

🗑️ Stock Scrap & Write-Off Q3 2026

Record scrapped or damaged stock with reason codes (damage, expiry, theft, admin error). All write-offs are logged in the movement audit trail and reduce stock value accordingly.

Stock Movements

A complete audit trail of every stock movement

Every unit that moves through your operation is logged: sales, receipts, adjustments, transfers, FBA inbounds, scraps, and manual corrections. The Stock Moves page gives you a searchable, filterable record of everything that's ever happened to your inventory.

  • Filter by product, location, movement type, or date range
  • Trace any discrepancy back to its source
  • Export full movement history for accounting reconciliation
  • Running stock-on-hand value per product at any point in time
