Cookie Policy

Last updated: 1 March 2026

This Cookie Policy explains what cookies are, how e-Lister uses them on this website, and how you can control them.

1. What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work efficiently and to provide information to website owners. Cookies do not contain personally identifiable information unless you have provided such information to the website.

2. How we use cookies

We use cookies on this website for the following purposes:

Strictly necessary cookies

These cookies are essential for the website to function correctly. They enable core functionality such as navigation and access to secure areas. The website cannot function properly without these cookies. You cannot opt out of these cookies.

Analytics cookies

We use analytics cookies to understand how visitors interact with our website — for example, which pages are visited most often, how long visitors stay, and where they came from. This helps us improve the website and its content. Analytics data is collected in aggregated, anonymised form wherever possible. Where analytics involves personal data (such as IP address), it is processed on the basis of our legitimate interests in improving our website.

Functionality cookies

These cookies allow the website to remember choices you make (such as whether you have dismissed a notice) and provide enhanced, more personal features. They may be set by us or by third-party providers whose services we have added to our pages.

3. Cookies we currently use

  • Session cookies — temporary cookies that expire when you close your browser. Used to maintain your session as you navigate between pages.
  • Preference cookies — remember settings or choices you have made (e.g. dismissing a banner). Persist for up to 12 months.
  • Analytics cookies — used to collect anonymised usage statistics. Persist for up to 26 months.

We do not currently use advertising or targeting cookies. We do not use cookies to track you across third-party websites.

4. Third-party cookies

Our website may load resources (such as fonts from Google Fonts) from third-party servers. These third parties may set their own cookies. We do not control these cookies and they are governed by the relevant third party's privacy policy.

Google Fonts: fonts are loaded from Google's CDN. Google may collect technical data. See Google's Privacy Policy for details.

5. Managing and disabling cookies

You can control and manage cookies in several ways:

  • Browser settings — all modern browsers allow you to view, manage, delete and block cookies. Refer to your browser's help documentation for instructions.
  • Opt-out tools — for analytics services, you may be able to opt out via browser extensions such as the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

Please note that disabling cookies may affect the functionality of this website and other websites you visit. If you disable strictly necessary cookies, parts of the website may not work correctly.

6. Changes to this policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time. The current version will always be available on this page with the "last updated" date shown at the top.

7. Contact us

If you have any questions about our use of cookies, please contact us at: privacy@e-lister.io
