Listing Capabilities

From catalogue to live listing — automatically

🧠 Claude & Gemini AI Generation

Choose between Claude (Anthropic) and Gemini (Google) models for listing generation. Our prompting layer is tuned for e-commerce copywriting — it understands keyword placement, channel tone, and conversion best practices out of the box.

📐 Schema-Driven Amazon Listings

Amazon product listings must conform to exact product-type schemas. MarketMate fetches Amazon's live schemas at submission time and validates every field before sending — so you never fail listing due to a missing required attribute or wrong data type.

⚙️ Smart Configurators

A configurator is a persistent mapping template that defines how your product data fields map to a channel's schema fields. Build one configurator per channel type and reuse it across your entire catalogue. AI can generate a configurator automatically from a sample product.

🔄 AI Configurator Setup

Not sure how to map your fields? Run the AI Configurator Setup wizard. Provide a sample product, select the target channel, and MarketMate's AI will propose a full field mapping — ready for you to review, tweak, and save as a reusable configurator.

📋 Bulk Listing Generation

Select your entire catalogue or any product subset and run AI generation in bulk. Review results before publishing, or set auto-publish rules for products that hit your quality threshold. Process thousands of listings in minutes, not days.

🌐 Per-Channel Optimisation

Each channel has different requirements: Amazon wants keyword-dense titles, eBay prefers structured descriptions, Etsy rewards storytelling copy. MarketMate's AI understands these differences and writes appropriately for each destination channel.

🏷️ Listing Health Scores

Every listing gets a health score based on title length, keyword density, image count, bullet point quality, and channel-specific requirements. Address issues before they impact your ranking or cause listing suppression.

🔍 Catalog ASIN Matching

When creating Amazon listings, MarketMate searches the Amazon catalog to find existing ASINs that match your product. Contribute to an existing ASIN or create a new one — the decision is yours, but the data is always fresh.

Channel Coverage

Dedicated listing forms for every channel

MarketMate has purpose-built listing creation pages for every supported channel — each rendering the exact fields that channel requires, with real-time validation against the channel's schema.
