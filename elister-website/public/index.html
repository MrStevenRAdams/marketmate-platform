Why we're different

The MarketMate difference

Other platforms bolt features on. We designed MarketMate as a single integrated system from day one — which means every module shares the same real-time data layer.

01

Schema-driven Amazon listings

We fetch Amazon's live product-type schemas at submission time, not at build time. That means your listings always conform to Amazon's latest requirements — automatically, without waiting for us to ship an update.

02

Sub-second inventory sync

Our sync engine processes stock changes as events, not batch jobs. When a sale happens on Amazon, your eBay, Etsy, and Kaufland listings are updated within seconds — not 15 minutes or an hour.

03

AI configurators that learn your data

Our AI doesn't just fill fields — it builds a persistent configurator that understands your product catalogue's structure. Over time, it gets smarter at mapping your data to channel schemas, cutting manual field-mapping work to near zero.

04

FBA & multi-location natively

Amazon FBA inbound shipments, bin rack management, inter-warehouse transfers, and 3PL fulfilment sources are all first-class features — not add-on modules. Your fulfilment network is modelled exactly as it is in real life.

05

No-code automation that actually works

Our visual workflow builder lets non-technical operators create rules that fire on any event in the system. Unlike competitors, our rules can span multiple modules — an order event can trigger a stock reorder, a carrier assignment, and a buyer message simultaneously.

06

Built-in SLA intelligence

MarketMate tracks each marketplace's dispatch SLA commitment and surfaces at-risk orders before they breach. No other platform in our category provides per-channel SLA tracking at the order level out of the box.
