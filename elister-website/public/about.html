    About MarketMate

    Built by sellers,
    for sellers

    MarketMate exists because we lived the problem. Juggling spreadsheets, copying order numbers between tabs, and losing sleep over missed SLAs — we've been there. So we built the platform we always wanted.

    Our story

    Why MarketMate exists

    The multichannel e-commerce space is full of tools that were built for a simpler era. Most inventory systems were designed when "multichannel" meant two marketplaces, "AI" wasn't a consideration, and Amazon's API was a fraction of its current complexity. Those tools have been bolted together, acquired, and patched for years — and the sellers using them pay the price.

    Marketmate Solutions Ltd was founded in the UK with a clear conviction: sellers deserve a platform that's as sophisticated as the marketplaces they operate on. Not a system that approximates Amazon's requirements, but one that speaks Amazon's language natively — fetching live product schemas at submission time, understanding SLA logic per channel, and syncing inventory in real time rather than on a batch cycle.

    "We didn't set out to build another order management tool. We set out to build the operating system for multichannel retail."

    We started with Amazon SP-API — the most complex, most important marketplace integration in e-commerce. Rather than wrapping it in abstractions that simplify it too far, we built tools that expose its full power: schema-driven listing builders, FBA inbound planning, performance monitoring, and SLA intelligence at the individual order level. Getting SP-API approved was a milestone, but it was just the beginning.

    From there, we built outwards — adding eBay, Etsy, OnBuy, Kaufland, Back Market, BigCommerce, Magento, WooCommerce, Bluepark, Temu, and TikTok Shop, each with the same depth of integration that Amazon received. No half-measures, no "partial" support. Every channel we connect to, we connect properly.

    Today, MarketMate manages orders, inventory, listings, shipping, analytics, and automation for sellers operating across more than a dozen channels — from a single, clean interface that doesn't require a PhD to use.

    What we stand for

    The principles that drive every product decision we make.

    🎯

    Depth over breadth

    We'd rather integrate with one channel properly than twelve channels poorly. Every integration we ship is full-featured — orders, inventory, listings, shipping, and messaging — not a read-only feed.

    ⚡

    Real time, not batch

    Inventory that syncs every 15 minutes is inventory that lies. Our event-driven architecture propagates stock changes sub-second across every connected channel — because overselling is never acceptable.

    🤖

    AI as an amplifier

    We use Claude and Gemini to do what AI is genuinely good at — generating compelling, optimised listing content — and keep humans in control of everything else. No black boxes, no unexplained decisions.

    🔒

    Trust through transparency

    Your data belongs to you. We maintain full audit trails, offer 90-day post-cancellation data access, and publish our policies in plain language rather than burying them in legal small print.

    🛠

    Operator-grade reliability

    Your business doesn't stop at 5pm. We run on Google Cloud's europe-west2 region with redundant infrastructure, comprehensive monitoring, and a team that takes uptime personally.

    🤝

    Partnership, not just software

    Our best features came from customer conversations. We maintain direct, open lines with every customer — and the feedback we receive directly shapes what we build next.

    Under the hood

    A platform built for scale

    Enterprise-grade infrastructure, chosen deliberately — not inherited from a previous era.

    Backend
    Go (Golang)
    High-performance, low-latency API services. Concurrency built in from the ground up.
    Infrastructure
    Google Cloud Platform
    Hosted in europe-west2 (London). GDPR-compliant by design, with global redundancy.
    Database

    Horizontally scalable NoSQL — handles inventory sync at high throughput without bottlenecks.

    Industry-standard authentication with MFA support, OAuth, and enterprise SSO (on roadmap).

    143 views, fully typed. Fast, reactive UI built for power users who live in the platform.
    AI
    Claude + Gemini
    Best-in-class language models for listing generation, with fallback logic and quality scoring.
