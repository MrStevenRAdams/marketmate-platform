Core Capabilities

Everything you need to master your order flow

🔍 Universal Search & Filtering

Search any order by order number, customer name, SKU, or internal reference. Filter by channel, status (Awaiting, On Hold, Dispatched, Cancelled), dispatch urgency, or date range. Save filters as quick views for your team.

⚡ SLA Urgency Indicators

Each order shows its channel's dispatch SLA commitment. Orders approaching their deadline turn amber, overdue orders turn red. The SLA Dashboard gives you a bird's-eye view of your daily dispatch performance across every channel.

📋 4-Tab Order Detail Drawer

Click any order to open a slide-out detail drawer without losing your place in the list. Four tabs cover: full Details (items, address, carrier), expanded Line Items, full Activity audit trail, and Notes (internal + channel notes).

🚚 Inline Dispatch Flow

Select carrier, choose service level with live rate comparison, enter package dimensions, generate label, and mark as dispatched — all within the order drawer without leaving the page. Dispatch status syncs back to the channel automatically.

📦 Bulk Processing

Select hundreds of orders at once. Bulk-mark as dispatched, bulk-print labels, bulk-put on hold, or bulk-export to CSV. Batch Process mode lets you process multiple orders through the same carrier and service in a single workflow.

🗂️ Orders Organisation

Assign orders to folders, apply searchable tags, add internal reference codes, and move orders to specific warehouse locations or fulfilment centres. Keep your team's workflow structured even at high volume.

🔀 Merge & Split Orders

Merge multiple orders from the same customer into a single shipment, or split one order across multiple parcels or fulfilment locations. Handle complex fulfilment scenarios without manual workarounds.

🖨️ Print Suite

Print invoices, shipping labels, pick lists, pack lists, and stock item labels directly from the platform. Batch-print labels for an entire wave of orders with one click. Compatible with Zebra, Dymo, and standard A4 printers.

Actions Menu

Six action groups. Every order scenario covered.

MarketMate's contextual Actions menu groups every possible order action into six logical submenus, available on individual orders or bulk selections.

📁

Organise

Folders, tags, reference codes, warehouse location assignment, fulfilment centre override.

📦

Items

Batch/serial number assignment, auto-assign batches, link unlinked items, add to purchase order.

🚚

Shipping

Change service, get live quotes, cancel label, split packaging, change dispatch and delivery dates.

🖨️

Print

Invoice, shipping label, pick list, pack list, stock item label — all from one menu.

▶️

Process Order

Single order processing flow or batch-process multiple selected orders simultaneously.

⚙️

Other Actions

Change status, view order notes, view raw XML, split order into separate orders, cancel, delete.

Processed Orders

Every dispatched order. Full history.

The Processed Orders view gives you a complete record of all dispatched and completed orders. Filter by date range, channel, or carrier. Re-print labels, view tracking information, and access the full activity trail.

  • Searchable history of all dispatched orders
  • Tracking number lookup and carrier status
  • Re-print label for any historical order
  • Export filtered order sets to CSV or XLSX
  • Delivery exception alerts linked back to original orders
