Platform Features

One platform. Every workflow covered.

MarketMate brings together order management, inventory, listings, shipping, analytics and automation into a single coherent system. No integrations to glue together. No data silos. Just one platform that runs your multichannel operation.

🛒

Order Management

Unified order inbox for every channel. SLA tracking, bulk processing, order detail drawer, notes, activity log, and inline dispatch flow. Every order from Amazon, eBay, Etsy, Temu, and more — in one place.

Full Details →
📦

Inventory & Warehousing

Real-time stock sync across unlimited locations, FBA, and 3PLs. Bin rack management, stock counts, inter-warehouse transfers, pick waves, replenishment, and full movement history.

Full Details →
🤖

AI Listing Tools

Generate optimised listings for Amazon, eBay, Etsy and every other channel using Claude and Gemini AI. Smart configurators automatically map your catalogue data to channel schemas.

Full Details →
🚚

Shipping & Dispatch

Live rate-shopping across Royal Mail, Evri, DPD, and FedEx. Book and print labels, manage manifests, track delivery exceptions, and monitor SLA compliance from a dedicated dashboard.

Full Details →
📊

Analytics & Forecasting

Revenue by channel, demand forecasting, inventory health, SLA compliance, operational KPIs, and a full reporting hub. Export to CSV or schedule automated report delivery.

Full Details →
⚡

Automation & Workflows

Visual no-code rule builder triggered on any platform event. Auto-route orders, assign carriers, send buyer messages, raise purchase orders — all without developer help.

💬

Buyer Messaging

Unified messaging inbox for all marketplace buyer communications. AI-assisted replies, message templates, and direct Amazon Messaging API integration via webhooks.

🔄

Supplier & Purchase Orders

Manage supplier records, raise and send purchase orders, receive goods against POs, and auto-trigger reorders when stock falls below configured thresholds.

📋

Product Information Management

Single master catalogue with full variant management, rich attribute sets, image management, and category hierarchy. Push product data to every connected channel instantly.

🏷️

Price Sync & Rules

Configure price rules per channel, apply multipliers or flat adjustments, and sync price changes across all listings automatically when your master price changes.

🔐

Multi-User & Permissions

Invite team members with role-based access control. Audit logs track every action across your account. Suitable for warehouse teams, office staff, and management alike.

📥

Import & Export

Bulk import products, stock levels, and prices from CSV or XLSX. Export any dataset with custom column mapping. Full data portability — your data is always yours.
