⏱ Advanced Analytics — Q3 2026

Analytics Modules

Seven analytics views. One complete picture.

📈 Revenue & Order Analytics

Revenue by channel, by day, by product, and by fulfilment method. Order volume trends, average order value, and return rates. Compare any period against the previous period to spot growth or decline.

📦 Inventory Health Dashboard

Stock levels by product and location, dead stock identification, days-of-supply calculation, and stock value by warehouse. Spot overstock and understock positions before they become problems.

🔮 Demand Forecasting Q3 2026

Machine-learning-based demand forecasts per product, updated daily. See predicted sell-through date, recommended reorder date, and suggested reorder quantity based on your configured lead times and safety stock targets.

⏱️ SLA Compliance Dashboard Q3 2026

Per-channel dispatch SLA compliance rate across any date range. Identify which channels, product types, or carrier services cause the most SLA breaches. Set compliance targets and receive alerts when rates drop below threshold.

⚙️ Operational KPI Dashboard

Throughput by hour and day, channel sync health status, team activity metrics, and automation rule performance. The operational view every warehouse manager and e-commerce director needs.

📊 Order Analytics Dashboard

Order volume by channel, by day of week, by fulfilment method (FBA vs MFN vs 3PL). Cancellation rates by channel. Average time from order receipt to dispatch. Identify your peak hours and staff accordingly.

📋 Reporting Hub Q3 2026

A central library of built-in report templates: stock valuation, P&L summary, carrier performance, channel comparison. Run any report ad-hoc, save custom filters as saved reports, and schedule automated CSV delivery by email.

🔍 Pivot Analytics Q3 2026

Build custom pivot tables across any combination of dimensions and metrics. Slice revenue by channel and product category simultaneously. Export raw pivot data to Excel for further analysis.
