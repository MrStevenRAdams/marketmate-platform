CONFIDENTIAL – INTERNAL AND REGULATORY USE

1. Purpose and Scope

This Data Retention Policy establishes the principles, schedules, and procedures governing how Marketmate Solutions Ltd retains, archives, and disposes of personal data and business records processed through the e-Lister order management platform.

This policy applies to:

  • All personal data processed by e-Lister on behalf of Customers (data processor obligation)
  • All personal data collected directly from website visitors and Customer representatives (data controller obligation)
  • All business records, audit logs, and operational data generated by the platform
  • All employees, contractors, and third-party processors who handle data on our behalf

2. Legal Framework

This policy is designed to comply with the following legislation and standards:

  • UK General Data Protection Regulation (UK GDPR)
  • Data Protection Act 2018
  • Amazon Selling Partner API (SP-API) Data Protection Policy
  • eBay Developer Programme Data Use Policy
  • Shopify Partner Data Use Requirements
  • Companies Act 2006 (financial record-keeping requirements)
  • HM Revenue and Customs record-keeping requirements

4. Data Minimisation

We collect and retain only the minimum personal data necessary for the purpose for which it was obtained. Specifically:

  • Buyer PII retrieved via marketplace APIs is used exclusively for order management, dispatch, and buyer messaging
  • We do not retain buyer PII beyond the periods specified in Section 3
  • We do not use buyer PII for marketing, profiling, or any purpose other than order fulfilment
  • Sensitive personal data is subject to field-level encryption using AES-256

5. Disposal Procedures

5.1 Anonymisation

Personal identifiers (name, email, address, phone) are replaced with anonymised placeholders. Order records are retained for financial compliance but can no longer be linked to an identifiable individual.

5.2 Secure Deletion

Data is permanently deleted from all primary storage, backup, and archive systems via the Admin SDK. Deletion is confirmed and logged.

5.3 Automatic Expiry

GCP retention policies and log sink expiry rules enforce automatic deletion without manual intervention.

6. Amazon SP-API Specific Requirements

  • Buyer PII is accessed only for purposes declared in our SP-API application: order fulfilment, dispatch label generation, and buyer messaging
  • Buyer PII is not shared with any third party except shipping carriers for the sole purpose of generating dispatch labels
  • Buyer PII is retained for no longer than 30 days after order fulfilment is confirmed (maximum 90 days where a return window applies)
  • All access to buyer PII via the Amazon SP-API is logged in GCP Cloud Audit Logs with Data Read events enabled on Firestore
  • We will notify Amazon and affected buyers within 72 hours of becoming aware of any personal data breach affecting Amazon buyer data

7. Data Subject Rights

Data subjects may exercise their rights under UK GDPR by contacting privacy@e-lister.co.uk. We will respond within one calendar month.

  • Right of access
  • Right to rectification
  • Right to erasure
  • Right to restriction
  • Right to data portability
  • Right to object
